Good to Know

Common questions.

What areas do you serve in Indiana?

We handle residential driveways across eastern Indiana and the I-70 corridor, and commercial paving and sealcoating projects throughout Indiana. Call us with your location and we'll confirm coverage.

When is the best time of year to pave or sealcoat?

Paving and sealcoating are warm-weather work, typically April through October. Asphalt and sealer need dry conditions and mild temperatures to cure properly, so we schedule around the forecast.

How long before I can drive on new asphalt or fresh sealcoat?

Fresh sealcoat generally needs about 24 to 48 hours before traffic, and new asphalt should cure before heavy or turning loads. We give you exact timing for your job and the weather at completion.

Do you handle commercial parking lots and line striping?

Yes. We pave, seal, crack-fill and stripe commercial lots, including stall layout, fire lanes and ADA markings. Our recent work includes large retail and high-traffic lots.
