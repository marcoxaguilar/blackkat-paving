FAQ

Common questions

What areas does Blackkat Paving serve?
We're based near Richmond, Indiana and serve Wayne, Henry, Franklin and Union counties in Indiana, plus Preble, Darke and western Montgomery counties in Ohio — including New Paris, Eaton, Greenville, New Castle and the surrounding towns. If you're near the state line, just ask.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes. Every commercial estimate is free. We'll visit the property, measure the lot, assess pavement condition and give you a clear, written proposal with scope and pricing — no obligation.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Blackkat Paving carries liability insurance and works to applicable Indiana and Ohio requirements. We're happy to provide a certificate of insurance for your facility or board records.
What's the difference between sealcoating and repaving?
Sealcoating is a protective top layer that extends the life of asphalt that's still structurally sound. Repaving (overlay or replacement) is needed when the surface or base has failed. We'll tell you honestly which one your lot actually needs.
How long does a commercial parking lot take?
Most commercial sealcoating and striping jobs finish in a day or two; resurfacing and new paving depend on square footage and phasing. We schedule around your operating hours — nights, weekends or phased lanes — so you stay open.
When is the best time of year to pave or sealcoat?
In the Indiana/Ohio climate, the prime window runs roughly April through October when temperatures stay warm enough for proper curing. We book the busy season early, so it pays to plan ahead.
