About Blackkat Paving

A local crew that would rather earn a good reputation than chase a quick sale.

Blackkat Paving is a locally owned asphalt paving company based near Richmond, Indiana. We work both sides of the state line — eastern Indiana and western Ohio — paving driveways for homeowners and lots for the churches, schools, apartment communities, warehouses and businesses that keep our towns running. Residential driveways are our specialty.

What we believe

Most people's experience with paving contractors isn't great. Quotes that don't add up, crews that disappear, work that fails in two winters. We started Blackkat to be the opposite of that: tell people the truth about what their lot needs, do the groundwork right, and stand behind it.

How we work

Every job starts with a real assessment and a written, itemized estimate — no vague numbers. We schedule around your operations, prep and compact the base properly instead of cutting corners under the surface, and walk the finished lot with you before we call it done.

Licensed & insured. We carry liability coverage and can provide a certificate of insurance for your facility or board on request.

How it works

From cracked to flawless in six steps

On-site assessment

We visit your property, measure the lot and assess pavement condition — then explain what it actually needs, not what's easiest to sell.

Written estimate

You get a clear, itemized proposal with scope, materials and pricing you can take to an owner or board.

Schedule around you

We plan the work around your hours — nights, weekends or phased lanes — so you keep operating.

Prep & base work

Cleaning, milling, repairs and base prep set the stage. Most lots fail from the bottom up, so we get this right.

Pave, seal & stripe

Hot-mix asphalt, sealcoat and crisp striping go down by a crew that does this every day.

Final walkthrough

We walk the finished lot with you, confirm every detail and leave the site clean.
