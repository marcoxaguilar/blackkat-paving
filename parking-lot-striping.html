Parking Lot Striping & ADA Layout

Crisp, compliant line striping, stalls, arrows and ADA accessible spaces.

Sharp striping maximizes stall count, directs traffic and keeps you compliant. We lay out and paint stalls, fire lanes, arrows, stop bars and ADA-accessible spaces with proper access aisles and signage — re-stripes and brand-new layouts alike.

Where we do it: Richmond & New Castle, IN · New Paris, Eaton & Greenville, OH · and every town across our 7-county service area.

What's included

  • ADA-compliant accessible stalls
  • High-traffic & reflective paint
  • Stall optimization & traffic flow
  • Fire lanes, arrows, stencils

How it works

From cracked to flawless in six steps

On-site assessment

We visit your property, measure the lot and assess pavement condition — then explain what it actually needs, not what's easiest to sell.

Written estimate

You get a clear, itemized proposal with scope, materials and pricing you can take to an owner or board.

Schedule around you

We plan the work around your hours — nights, weekends or phased lanes — so you keep operating.

Prep & base work

Cleaning, milling, repairs and base prep set the stage. Most lots fail from the bottom up, so we get this right.

Pave, seal & stripe

Hot-mix asphalt, sealcoat and crisp striping go down by a crew that does this every day.

Final walkthrough

We walk the finished lot with you, confirm every detail and leave the site clean.
